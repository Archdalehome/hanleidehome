Why we can produce a cozy and long lasting sofa?

Pure-natural Fiber Fabrics

We exclusively use pure cotton and linen fabrics for our sofas to ensure excellent skin comfort and touch. These natural fibers allow the sofa to breathe, providing a comfortable seating experience in all seasons while maintaining durability and elegance over time.

All fabrics undergo rigorous testing and inspection in our factory before being approved for production. Our comprehensive quality control includes color fastness tests to ensure consistent appearance over time and Martindale abrasion tests to verify durability and resistance to wear. Only fabrics that meet our strict standards are used in our furniture.

High Strength Frame Construction

Our sofa frames are crafted from solid hardwood using traditional mortise and tenon joinery techniques. This construction method ensures the frame maintains its structural integrity over years of use without warping or weakening. Additionally, all our wood materials meet strict environmental standards, making our furniture both durable and eco-friendly.

Durable Filling Materials

We use premium high-density, high-resilience foam with densities ranging from D30 to D40 for our cushions. Each batch of foam undergoes internal testing and inspection to verify its performance characteristics. This rigorous quality control ensures our sofas maintain excellent resilience and comfort even after years of daily use, preventing sagging and maintaining the original shape and support.
