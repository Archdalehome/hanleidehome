About Hanleide Home Textiles

Company Profile

Hanleide Home Textiles (HHT) is a leading enterprise specializing in high-end home textiles. We are dedicated to perfectly combining comfort, style, and quality, focusing on the research and development of premium linen-cotton blended home fabrics and their derivative home products. Our mission is to bring an exquisite blend of sophistication and practicality to every living space.

Product Portfolio

Our product line encompasses a carefully curated selection of home furnishings: from soft, comfortable pillows that perfectly end your day; to elegant cushions that add comfort and style to any seating; to beautiful blankets that provide warmth on cold nights; and sophisticated napkins that enhance your dining experience. Our curtains not only beautify your windows but also provide privacy protection and light control, comprehensively elevating your home environment.

Business Model

HHT operates on a dual business model:

1. We provide premium OEM services to high-end home furnishing clients worldwide. Our modern production facilities are staffed with skilled artisans and technicians who ensure every product meets the most stringent standards. Understanding the importance of brand identity, we work closely with our partners to transform design concepts into final products.

2. We collaborate with internationally renowned fabric manufacturers in development and sales. This synergy allows us to combine our expertise in linen-cotton materials with global innovative design concepts, launching trend-setting products that set new standards in home decoration with unique textures, patterns, and functionality.

Quality Commitment

Our commitment to quality permeates every aspect of our operations. We select only the finest linen and cotton fibers to ensure durability, breathability, and luxurious feel in our products. Strict quality control measures are implemented at every production stage, from raw material inspection to finished product testing. This dedication to quality has earned us the trust and loyalty of our customers, making us their preferred partner in home textiles.
