About Hanleide Furniture - OEM/ODM Upholstery Furniture Manufacturer

Hanleide Furniture is a professional OEM/ODM upholstery furniture manufacturer based in Haining City, Zhejiang Province, China - the renowned "Leather City" with a deep heritage in furniture craftsmanship. We are dedicated to producing high-quality custom fabric upholstery furniture for retailers, importers and wholesalers worldwide.

Manufacturing Scale & Capacity

Our 20,000-square-meter modern production facility is designed for efficiency, featuring optimized workshops, dedicated storage areas, and strict quality control zones. We operate with a strong monthly capacity of 200 containers, enabling us to handle large-volume, time-sensitive orders while maintaining rigorous quality standards across every shipment. Our factory has established a solid presence in major global markets including North America, Australia, Europe, and the Middle East.

Our Craftsmanship

We carefully select the finest home furnishing materials and bring them to life by hand-crafting each piece using techniques passed down from generations of skilled artisans. We carry on this tradition by selecting only the finest fabrics made from pure natural fibers, blending them with eight-way hand-tied solid wood frames - a hallmark of premium upholstery construction. Every piece is painstakingly built and tailored to your exact specifications in our partnered factories. The result is unparalleled custom-made fabric upholstery designed and built to fit your business needs.

Our Business Model

As a dedicated OEM/ODM manufacturer, we sell our products to retailers, importers, and wholesalers globally. We do NOT sell to individual consumers, ensuring our partners receive factory-direct pricing and dedicated B2B support.
